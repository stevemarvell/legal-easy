EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on March 15, 2022, between TechCorp Solutions Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Company"), and Sarah Chen ("Employee").

POSITION AND DUTIES
Employee is hereby employed as Senior Safety Engineer. Employee shall perform such duties and responsibilities as are customarily associated with such position, including but not limited to:
- Conducting safety assessments and audits of company facilities
- Developing and implementing safety protocols and procedures
- Investigating workplace incidents and preparing safety reports
- Ensuring compliance with OSHA and other regulatory requirements
- Training employees on safety procedures and best practices
- Reporting safety violations and concerns to management

TERM OF EMPLOYMENT
This is an at-will employment relationship. Either party may terminate this employment at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice, subject to the provisions below.

NOTICE PROVISION
In the event of termination by either party, thirty (30) days written notice shall be provided, except in cases of termination for cause, which may be immediate.

COMPENSATION
Employee's initial annual salary shall be $95,000, payable in accordance with Company's standard payroll practices. Employee shall be eligible for annual performance reviews and salary adjustments at Company's discretion.

BENEFITS
Employee shall be entitled to participate in Company's standard benefits package, including health insurance, dental insurance, vision insurance, 401(k) plan, and paid time off in accordance with Company policies.

CONFIDENTIALITY
Employee acknowledges that during employment, Employee may have access to confidential and proprietary information. Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all such information during and after employment.

SAFETY REPORTING OBLIGATIONS
Employee has a duty and right to report safety violations, hazardous conditions, or regulatory non-compliance to appropriate management personnel or regulatory authorities without fear of retaliation. Company prohibits any form of retaliation against employees who make good faith safety reports.

GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
